Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's a simple yet rich and flavorful recipe that's loved for its creamy texture and savory taste.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, saut√© diced pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan, and a pinch of black pepper.
  4. Drain cooked spaghetti and quickly toss it in the egg and cheese mixture.
  5. Add the crispy pancetta, ensuring the pasta is coated evenly.
  6. Season with salt and more black pepper if desired.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan and fresh parsley.
