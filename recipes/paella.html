Paella

Description

Paella is a famous Spanish dish originating from Valencia. It's a flavorful rice dish cooked with a variety of ingredients, such as saffron, vegetables, seafood, and/or meat, creating a delicious one-pan meal.

Ingredients

  • Short-grain rice
  • Chicken, rabbit, and/or seafood (such as shrimp and mussels)
  • Onion, chopped
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Tomatoes, diced
  • Chicken broth
  • Saffron threads
  • Paprika
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the short-grain rice under cold water and set aside.
  2. In a large paella pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and sliced bell peppers, cooking until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes and cook for a few minutes until they start to break down.
  5. Add the short-grain rice to the pan, stirring to coat the grains with the vegetable mixture.
  6. In a separate pot, heat chicken broth with saffron threads and paprika. Once heated, pour the broth over the rice in the paella pan.
  7. Add the chicken, rabbit, and/or seafood on top of the rice, distributing evenly.
  8. Simmer over medium heat until the rice is cooked, and the liquid is absorbed, creating a flavorful crust on the bottom known as "socarrat."
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve the paella directly from the pan, garnished with fresh herbs like parsley.
