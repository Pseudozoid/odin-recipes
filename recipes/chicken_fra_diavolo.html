Chicken Fra Diavolo

Description

Chicken Fra Diavolo is a spicy and flavorful Italian-American dish that typically features chicken cooked in a spicy tomato and pepper sauce. The name "Fra Diavolo" translates to "Brother Devil" in Italian, indicating the dish's characteristic spiciness.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (such as thighs, drumsticks, or breast pieces)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (to add heat)
  • Tomatoes, either diced or crushed
  • Tomato paste
  • White wine
  • Chicken broth
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Onion, sliced
  • Italian herbs and spices (such as oregano, basil, and thyme)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Pasta (commonly served with the dish)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and brown them on all sides. Remove the chicken and set it aside.
  3. In the same pan, add more olive oil if needed. Saut√© minced garlic and crushed red pepper flakes until fragrant.
  4. Stir in sliced onions and bell peppers, cooking until they start to soften.
  5. Pour in white wine and let it simmer for a couple of minutes to reduce.
  6. Add diced or crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, chicken broth, and a combination of Italian herbs. Stir well to combine.
  7. Return the browned chicken to the pan, making sure to coat each piece with the sauce. Cover and simmer over medium-low heat until the chicken is cooked through, and the flavors meld together.
  8. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. If you prefer a spicier dish, you can add more red pepper flakes.
  9. Serve the Chicken Fra Diavolo over cooked pasta, allowing the spicy tomato sauce to coat the chicken and pasta.
  10. Garnish with fresh herbs like chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
